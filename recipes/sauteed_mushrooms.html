Easy Sauteed Mushrooms

Description

Savoury soyed mushrooms. Perfect side dish, for something like eggs or steak.

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • mushrooms, sliced to preference
  • soy sauce, to taste

Steps

  1. Pour 1 tbsp oil onto hot pan.
  2. Dump mushrooms in, stir continuously. If oil does not coat all mushrooms, add more. Only coat enough, do not over-add surplus of olive oil
  3. When mushrooms all coated with oil, add soy sauce to taste. Stir until water has evaporated enough.
  4. Dump and serve.
